5x5 grid of platforms connected by destructible bridges. The outer ones lack spawn locations for players.
Center=Center platform
I-Inner
O-Outer
M-Medium
L-Left
R-Left
U-Upper
B-Backing

OMRU = Outer Medium Right Upper
References the one right above the outer right.
OFLB = Outer Far Left Backing
References the far left back corner.
OLB = Outer Left Backing
References the one just left of the back piece.
OF = Outer Front
References the one all the way in the front.

Platforms should be placed 28.25 apart, with bridges inbetween.
